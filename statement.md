Gift Management Inventory System

Problem Statement:
Small gift shops and individual sellers struggle with manual inventory tracking, leading to stock discrepancies, lost sales from out-of-stock items, and inefficient product management. The absence of affordable, simple digital solutions forces businesses to rely on error-prone manual methods, resulting in time wastage and potential revenue loss.

Scope of the Project:

Develop a standalone command-line application for inventory management

Implement basic CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations for gift items

Provide real-time stock level monitoring and updates

Focus on core inventory functions without advanced features like user authentication or database persistence

Support single-user operation with menu-driven interface

Target Users:

Small gift shop owners and managers

Individual gift sellers and artisans

Store employees handling daily inventory operations

Students learning inventory management systems

Small business owners needing basic stock tracking

High-Level Features:

Inventory Management: Add, remove, and modify gift item records

Stock Control: Increase/decrease stock quantities with real-time updates

Quick Search: Instant item lookup by unique ID for availability and pricing

Complete Reporting: Generate full inventory lists with all product details

Data Validation: Input type checking and error handling for reliable operations

User-Friendly Interface: Simple menu-driven system requiring minimal training
